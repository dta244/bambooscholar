Methodology

How we count publications and citations for Vietnamese research output.

Counting Rule

A publication is counted as Vietnamese-affiliated if at least one author lists an institution in Vietnam (authorships.institutions.country_code: VN) in the OpenAlex database.

Citation counts use the cited_by_count field from each matching work, representing lifetime citations indexed by OpenAlex.

Document Types

All OpenAlex work types are included (articles, reviews, conference papers, book chapters, etc.). This provides the broadest coverage of Vietnamese research output.

Data Source

OpenAlex — a free, open catalog of the global research system. OpenAlex indexes over 250 million works from multiple sources including Crossref, PubMed, institutional repositories, and more.

Update schedule: Every Monday at 08:00 UTC via GitHub Actions.

Field Classification

Each work is assigned to exactly one field via OpenAlex's primary_topic.field. This prevents double-counting across disciplines — the sum of all field counts equals the national total.

Known Limitations

  • Diaspora effect: Only research produced within Vietnam is captured. Globally prominent scholars of Vietnamese descent at international institutions are not counted unless they also list a Vietnamese affiliation.
  • Institution double-counting: A paper co-authored by researchers at two different Vietnamese institutions counts once per institution. National totals use deduplication by work ID; institution-level totals do not.
  • Author truncation: OpenAlex truncates authorship lists at 100 entries. First-author checks are safe (position 0 is always present), but middle/last positions may be affected for mega-authored papers.
  • Citation lag: Newly published papers take weeks to months to accumulate citations. Recent-year citation totals will be underestimates.
  • Coverage gaps: OpenAlex does not index all grey literature, some conference-only venues, or certain predatory journals. Counts will differ from Scopus or Web of Science.
  • counts_by_year vs cited_by_count: These fields may not sum identically due to OpenAlex indexing updates.
